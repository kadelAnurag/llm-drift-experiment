Financial Products Eligibility Policy

Overview
This document defines eligibility criteria and decision logic for StarterCard, FlexiCard, and Personal Loan products. Applicants are evaluated based on income, savings, risk profile, employment type, income pattern, cashflow consistency, and documentation type. All thresholds are exclusive — an applicant must exceed (not merely meet) the stated minimum values to satisfy a condition.

---

StarterCard Policy

Rule 1 — Standard Salaried Applicants
Applies to: Salaried employees with stable, formally documented monthly income.
- Monthly income must exceed INR 20,000
- Total savings must exceed INR 50,000
- Allowed risk bands: Low Risk, Medium Risk
- High Risk applicants are not eligible
- Required income pattern: Monthly Recurring Income
- Required cashflow consistency: Highly Consistent Cashflow
- Accepted documentation: Formal Payslip, Bank Statement
- Income stability category: Stable Income
- Decision: Approve

Rule 2 — Alternative Income (Freelancer / Self-Employed)
Applies to: Freelancers and self-employed applicants with moderately stable but demonstrable income.
- Monthly income must exceed INR 15,000
- Total savings must exceed INR 50,000
- Allowed risk bands: Low Risk, Medium Risk
- High Risk applicants are not eligible
- Required income pattern: Monthly Recurring Income or Irregular But Consistent Income
- Required cashflow consistency: Highly Consistent Cashflow or Moderately Consistent Cashflow
- Accepted documentation: Bank Statement, Alternative Documentation
- Income stability category: Moderately Stable Income
- Decision: Approve

---

FlexiCard Policy

Rule — Standard (Salaried / Self-Employed)
Applies to: Salaried and self-employed applicants with higher financial capacity.
- Monthly income must exceed INR 50,000
- Total savings must exceed INR 100,000
- Allowed risk bands: Low Risk, Medium Risk
- High Risk applicants are not eligible
- Required income pattern: Monthly Recurring Income or Irregular But Consistent Income
- Required cashflow consistency: Highly Consistent Cashflow or Moderately Consistent Cashflow
- Accepted documentation: Bank Statement, Invoice-Based Documentation
- Income stability category: Stable Income
- Decision: Approve

---

Personal Loan Policy

Rule — Salaried and Gig Workers
Applies to: Salaried employees and gig workers with demonstrated repayment capacity.
- Monthly income must exceed INR 40,000
- Total savings must exceed INR 50,000
- Allowed risk bands: Low Risk, Medium Risk
- High Risk applicants are not eligible
- Required income pattern: Monthly Recurring Income or Irregular But Consistent Income
- Accepted documentation: Invoice-Based Documentation, Alternative Documentation
- Income stability category: Stable Income or Moderately Stable Income
- Decision: Approve

---

Decision Logic
- If all conditions for an applicable rule are satisfied → Approve
- If any key condition (income, savings, or risk band) fails → Reject
- If income and risk band conditions are met but savings are borderline (above threshold but marginal), the application may be flagged for Review
- Review decisions require human assessment before a final outcome is issued

---

Supporting Signals
Disposable income and predicted risk score are recorded for each assessment and may support human review, but do not override the primary eligibility rule conditions.
